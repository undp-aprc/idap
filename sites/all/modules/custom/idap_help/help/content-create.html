1. Navigate to http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add (this page is accessible from the 'Share Content' link in the top left-hand corner of the page).
  2. Choose the type of content you wish to create from the list by clicking on it's link. Alternatively, you may use the following links to navigate to the content creation pages directly.
    • Blog http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/blog
    • Country Activity http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/country-activity
    • E-Discussion http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/e-discussion
    • Event http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/event
    • Jobs and Opportunities http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/opportunities
    • Media Article http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/media-article
    • Network News http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/network-news
    • Page http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/page
    • Resource http://www.inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net/node/add/resource
  3. The fields in the left-hand column vary according to the type of content you are creating. Fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are required and must be completed before you can submit the form. Fields without an asterisk are optional.
  4. Fields in the right-hand column (in the orange box) are used to control the position of the content within the site structure. Choose a Development Theme for the content, from the 'theme' box. You may choose a country, if the content relates to a particular country.

    If you are a member of any 'Collaboration Spaces' you may also choose to post the content to your Collaboration Spaces (this option is hidden if you currently have no collaboration space memberships.

  5. When you have finished filling in the form's fields, press the 'Publish' button to save and publish your content.
