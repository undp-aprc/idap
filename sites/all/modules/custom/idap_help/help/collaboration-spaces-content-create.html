1. From the Collaboration Space page, click the type of content you would like to create from the 'Create Content' box on the left.
  2. Complete the content creation form as normal.
  3. In the 'Collaboration Spaces' box under 'Content Audience Options' you will notice that Collaboration Space is automatically selected. It is recommended to not change this selection.
  4. Check the 'Space Content Visibility' option is set correctly before publishing your content.
