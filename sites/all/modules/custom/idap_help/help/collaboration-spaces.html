Collaboration Spaces allow members of inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net to create virtual groups within which they can share content with other group members. Collaboration Spaces have the following benefits:

  • Can choose whether to make a Collaboration Space private or public.
  • Can share content within the context of the group and prevent non-group members from seeing it.
  • Collaboration Space managers have full control over inviting and removing members from the group.
  • Members of inclusivedevelopmentasiapacific.net who are members of several groups can easily cross-post content into any group of which they are a member.
