1. Navigate to a piece of content, such as a blog or a resource.
  2. Click the 'Workflow' tab.
  3. Choose the state to which the content should be assigned. For example, to change a piece of content from 'draft', choose 'final'.
  4. Under the 'schedule' you can either set the content to publish immediately, or it can be scheduled for a particular time and date (useful for content that has a publishing embargo).
  5. You can make a brief comment about your reasons for changing the content's state. This is stored in that piece of content's workflow log.
  6. Click 'Update Workflow' to save your changes.
