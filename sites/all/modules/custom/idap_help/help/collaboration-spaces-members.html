Add members to a Collaboration Space

  1. From the Collaboration Space page, click 'Add a member'.
  2. In the 'User name' textbox, start typing the username of the member you wish to add.
  3. From the list of matches, which are displayed under the 'User name' text box, click the name of the user you wish to add.
  4. Click 'Add Users' to add user to the Collaboration Space.

Remove members from a Collaboration Space

  1. From the Collaboration Space page, click the 'Group' tab.
  2. From the 'Group' menu, choose 'People'.
  3. From the list of group members, tick the members you would like to remove from the Collaboration Space.
  4. Under 'Operations' choose 'remove from group', then click 'execute'.
  5. Click 'Confirm' to remove the selected members from the Collaboration Space.
