1. When you are logged in, you will see a set of tabs at the top of landing pages, such as the homepage, including 'view' and 'edit' tabs.
  2. To place blocks on the page, click the 'edit' tab.
  3. You will see 'blocks' on the page (labelled as 'Block 1', 'Block 2', etc).
  4. To place a new block (or change an existing block), click a drop down list box to display a list of all the blocks that can be placed in the page.
  5. Choose the block you wish to place by clicking one of the options in the list.
  6. Click the 'Publish' button at the bottom of the page to save your changes and return to the live view of the page. Check your block is saved in the correct position and is displaying the content you expected.
